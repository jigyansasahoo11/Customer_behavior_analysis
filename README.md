# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 About the Project

This is my **first end-to-end Data Analytics project**, where I analyzed customer shopping behavior to uncover meaningful business insights. The project covers the complete analytics workflow—from data cleaning and exploration to SQL-based business analysis and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in working with real-world data, writing SQL queries, creating visualizations, and presenting insights that can support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchasing patterns and answer business questions such as:

- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- How do subscription status and discounts affect purchasing behavior?
- Which products are the most popular and highest rated?
- How does customer spending vary across different age groups and categories?

## 📈 Key Insights

- Identified top-selling and highest-rated products.
- Analyzed customer spending based on gender, age group, and subscription status.
- Compared revenue across different product categories.
- Evaluated the impact of discounts and shipping types on customer purchases.
- Created customer segments based on purchase history.
